## 🧠 Overview

**Content Warning** is a cooperative multiplayer horror game focused on exploration, survival, and recording terrifying encounters inside abandoned and dangerous environments. Players work together as content creators attempting to capture frightening footage while navigating unpredictable threats hidden in dark underground locations.

The game combines suspense-driven exploration with physics-based interactions and cooperative gameplay mechanics. Teams descend into hostile areas equipped with cameras, flashlights, and limited equipment, attempting to survive long enough to document supernatural creatures and disturbing events for online fame.

Every expedition presents new dangers, randomized encounters, and unpredictable environmental hazards that force players to communicate and adapt quickly. The deeper players venture into dangerous locations, the greater the rewards become — but survival grows increasingly difficult as terrifying entities emerge from the darkness.

A central gameplay element revolves around recording gameplay footage using in-game cameras. Successfully captured clips contribute to progression systems and create memorable moments that combine horror, humor, and chaotic teamwork. The game’s unique blend of tension and comedic multiplayer interactions creates highly replayable sessions.

Content Warning features immersive sound design, atmospheric lighting, and stylized visuals that amplify suspense during exploration. Cooperative coordination becomes essential as players manage resources, escape threats, and attempt to return safely with valuable recorded footage.

Whether exploring abandoned structures, escaping terrifying creatures, or creating chaotic multiplayer moments with friends, **Content Warning** delivers an intense and entertaining horror experience for Windows players.
